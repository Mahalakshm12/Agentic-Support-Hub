CUSTOMER SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE - COMPLETE GUIDE:

=== ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT ===
Password Reset:
- Visit https://app.smartsupport.com/reset-password
- Enter registered email → Check inbox/spam (24hr expiry)
- No email received? Check spam/junk → Still no? Contact support@smartsupport.com

2FA Setup:
- Account > Security > Enable 2FA
- Scan QR code with Google Authenticator/Authy
- Backup codes: Save 10 codes securely (one-time use)

Account Deletion:
- Account > Settings > Delete Account (30-day recovery)
- All data permanently deleted after 30 days

Email Updates:
- Account > Notifications → Toggle email preferences
- Unsubscribe: Footer of any email → "Manage Preferences"

=== PAYMENTS & BILLING ===
Payment Failed:
- Update card: Account > Billing > Payment Methods
- Retry after 24hrs if declined (bank hold)
- Supported: Visa, MC, Amex, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay

Subscription Management:
- Cancel anytime: Account > Billing > Cancel (immediate effect)
- Upgrade/downgrade: Same page → Changes at billing cycle end
- Refund policy: 7 days for annual plans

Invoice History:
- Account > Billing > Invoices (PDF download)
- Tax receipts available for 7 years

=== RETURNS & WARRANTY ===
Return Policy:
- 30 days for unopened items, original packaging
- 14 days for damaged/defective (photos required)
- Process: Orders > Request Return > Print prepaid label (free)

Warranty Claims:
- Lifetime warranty on hardware defects
- Submit: Support > Warranty > Upload photos + receipt
- Replacement ships within 48hrs

Exchange Policy:
- Same item, different size/color: Free exchange
- Different item: Treated as return + new purchase

=== SHIPPING & DELIVERY ===
Tracking Orders:
- Orders > Track Order (real-time updates)
- Standard: 3-5 days, Express: 1-2 days
- Delays? Check "Shipping Updates" email

International Shipping:
- 50+ countries, duties paid by customer
- Track via local carrier (DHL/FedEx/UPS)

Delivery Issues:
- Wrong address? Contact within 24hrs
- Stolen package? File police report + claim form

=== PRODUCT SUPPORT ===
Software Updates:
- Auto-update enabled by default
- Manual: App > Settings > Check for Updates
- Changelog: https://smartsupport.com/changelog

Troubleshooting:
- App crashes: Clear cache → Account > Advanced > Clear Data
- Slow performance: Close background apps + restart
- Connection issues: Toggle WiFi/Cellular → VPN off

API Access:
- Developer? https://api.smartsupport.com/docs
- Rate limits: 1000 calls/hour (Enterprise: unlimited)

=== CONTACT & ESCALATION ===
Live Chat: 24/7 → Bottom-right chat bubble
Phone: +1-800-SUPPORT (9AM-6PM EST)
Email: support@smartsupport.com (48hr response)

Priority Support:
- Enterprise accounts: Dedicated rep + 2hr response
- Request upgrade: Account > Support > Priority Request

=== LEGAL & PRIVACY ===
Privacy Policy: https://smartsupport.com/privacy
Terms of Service: https://smartsupport.com/terms
GDPR Compliance: EU residents → Data export available

Data Export:
- Account > Privacy > Download My Data (JSON format)
- Delete all data: Account > Privacy > Erase Everything
